About This Website

Hello, I'm a passionate photographer by the name Michael David Franklyn.

Photography is a way of feeling, touching, and loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever. It remembers the little things, long after you have forgotten everything.

Welcome to my collection of favorite moments, where every photo tells a story and every story is a memory cherished.

What you'll find on this site:

  • Gallery: A curated selection of my favorite and recent photos, organized in a modern grid layout.
  • About: Learn more about my journey and philosophy as a photographer.
  • Design: Review the original design and structure of this website.
  • Contact: Get in touch with me for collaborations, inquiries, or just to say hello!
